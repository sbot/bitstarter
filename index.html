What if you had even faster small parcel delivery service in your city? With Airrier, a self-navigating quadcopter carries your item directly to you, regardless of current automobile or foot traffic conditions. Imagine delivery from the window of one high-rise building to another. No need for your delivery to be sent downstairs, through traffic by bike messenger, and back upstairs to the recipient. That food, coffee, or emergency package is delivered much sooner and more directly - to her window!

How does it work? Simply use our app on your smartphone. If you wish to send a small parcel, the push of a button will dispatch a copter to you. Your phone will sound when our copter gets close to you, so you can hold your phone as a beacon to lead the copter to the landing spot you choose. Attach your package, enter the address and hit Send on your phone. In minutes your delivery will be on its way.

Proceeds from every delivery go to Airrier Africa, a network of courier copters we are creating to unite villages where roads are poor to non-existent. Our solar-powered copters are used to deliver cellphones, communication equipment, and crucial medical supplies between villages to turn them into hubs in a more prosperous countryside. Copters deliver the building materials for their own village ports. Once a village port is ready, the progress continues to other villages within flying distance.
